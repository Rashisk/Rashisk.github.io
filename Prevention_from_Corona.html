Prevention is better than cure !

So, Dear people, we all have seen the positive as well as the negative effects of this Devastating Pandemic !. In order to protect ourselves and let this disease wipe out completely from our life and all over the world, lets follow some rules and keep ourselves healthy .

Lets Start !

1. Wash your hands neatly with soap for at least 20 seconds !

 

2. Avoid touching your Mouth, Nose and Eyes !

3. Cover your mouth with elbow or tissue !

4. Wear a mask to protect yourself, even if you are not sick

5. Avoid going to public places unless its necessary! And practise Social distancing.

6. Stay safe at home/
