What you can do for others in this lockdown?

Many people still don't know the importance of this lockdown; also, many people are still out on the roads going to their hometowns, walking, due to lack of transport facility.You can do the following things which are possible like:

  • Reach out to people via calling and make them understand the importance of lockdown and tell them to stay at their homes unless necessary.
  • Help those people who are going to their hometowns walking by giving them some food, water and other essential things.
  • Donate money in PM Cares and other fundraisers for the people who are in need of help.
  • Don't cut the daily wages of the worker on your site, maid in your house and salary of your employee working from home!. They too are suffering along with you..

We now hope that you have understood the effects of this disease and you will also forward it to others for creating awareness about this harmful disease!
