Covid-19... What about the Future?

As we all know, the coronavirus & Covid-19 has caused a lot of loss and damage to humanity, its very important to fight back against it! There has been a huge crisis in economy all over the world. The world has come to a halt like situation after this tragedy. Also, the Government of India has announced lockdown since 24th March 2020. If we follow the rules and guidelines issued by the government, we can surely help them and ourselves to get out of this horrible situation the world is facing right now!

Why did the Government impose lockdown?

Since there is no vaccine to fight COVID-19, the government has stressed that social distancing is the only way to check the spread of the virus. Lockdown is considered the best way to keep people indoors.

Why is the lockdown and social distancing important?



As we all know, The confirmed cases and fatalities of the Covid-19 pandemic are rising exponentially. To prevent the outbreak, social distancing is utmost important. This can be achieved by overall country and world lockdown only. Though the government of many countries has taken this decision after facing a huge loss of people and economy, India too, is late but still early to impose lockdown over the whole country. Its a good sign of the lockdown that India hasn't entered its 3rd phase yet and we all pray that this should never happen. Thus , we all must follow social distancing amid this lockdown period and fight back against it.
